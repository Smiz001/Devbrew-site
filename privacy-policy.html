Privacy Policy

DevBrew
Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

DevBrew ("the App") is developed and maintained by an independent developer ("Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data You Enter

During normal use, you may enter or paste various types of data into the App, including but not limited to: JSON, JWT tokens, timestamps, URLs, Base64-encoded strings, hashes, regular expressions, color codes, and other developer-related content.

All of this data is processed entirely on your device. None of it is collected, transmitted, or accessible to the Developer or any third party.

Analytics & Telemetry

The App collects anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics via TelemetryDeck. This data helps the Developer understand which tools are used and improve app stability.

What is collected:

  • App version and build number
  • macOS version and device model
  • Screen resolution, locale, and accessibility settings
  • Which tools are opened and how often
  • Anonymous performance metrics (e.g. processing duration)
  • Error types (never including your input data)

What is never collected:

  • Clipboard content or any data you enter into the App
  • File contents or file paths
  • Your name, email, or any personally identifiable information
  • IP addresses (TelemetryDeck does not store them)

All identifiers are double-hashed and cannot be linked back to you personally. You can opt out of analytics at any time in the App's Settings.

Third-Party Services

The App uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous usage analytics. TelemetryDeck is a privacy-first analytics platform that does not collect personally identifiable information or IP addresses.

For more information, see the TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy.

The App does not use any advertising or third-party tracking services.

Updates

The App is distributed and updated exclusively through the Apple App Store. No background update mechanisms or network connections are used by the App itself beyond the analytics described above.

Data Storage

All app data (history, settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device. The Developer has no access to this data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
