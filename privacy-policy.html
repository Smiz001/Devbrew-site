Privacy Policy

DevBrew
Last updated: February 14, 2026

Overview

DevBrew ("the App") is developed by Nikita Posokhov ("Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data You Enter

During normal use, you may enter or paste various types of data into the App, including but not limited to: JSON, JWT tokens, timestamps, URLs, Base64-encoded strings, hashes, regular expressions, color codes, and other developer-related content.

All of this data is processed entirely on your device. None of it is collected, transmitted, or accessible to the Developer or any third party.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry systems in the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Updates

The App is distributed and updated exclusively through the Apple App Store. No background update mechanisms or network connections are used by the App itself.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. The Developer has no access to this data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
